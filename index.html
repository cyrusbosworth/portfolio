    About Me

    Hi, my name is Cyrus and I create beautiful interactive web apps. A few of my greatest loves in life are technology, creating art, and a good puzzle. Combine these things and I believe you will find web development. Building web pages and apps lets me combine my passion for creativity and design with logic and problem solving. Ever since learning BASIC on my first computer as a little kid, and building my first website at eleven years old, I’ve been hooked. My preferred stack is the MERN stack (Mongo, Express, React, Node), but one of the best things about web development is the breadth of technologies available. As a lover of learning, I am always excited to dive into new technologies, frameworks and libraries. I’d love to hear what you want to build, so contact me and let’s make something cool.
